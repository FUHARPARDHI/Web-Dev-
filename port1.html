RADIO FREQUENCY IDENTIFICATION TECHNOLOGY TRACKS STUDENTS ATTENDANCE

An RFID attendance system utilizes radio frequency identification technology to track students attendance, enhancing security ,efficiency and communication wuth educational institutions.When individuals are inside the RFID attendance reader's radiofrequency range,the system uses wireless identification to identify them.The RFID tag that carries their name,ID number ,class and section must be worn by both students and staff in order for the attedance to be automatically recorded. The receiver/reader of the RFID attendance system automatically logs the attendance and saves the attendance data in the ERP system.For the purpose of managing faculty salaries and payroll, the administrator can at any moment extract the data to acquire a summary attendance history and keep track of it.
